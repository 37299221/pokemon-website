Evolution

What is evolution in Pokemon?

Evolution in pokemon is the main way of making a pokemon stronger. This involves taking up a new form. Pokemon can evolve in a number of ways. These are by:

  • Leveling up via experience points (exp.)
  • Use of an item
  • Friendship (Or affection in rare cases).
  • Trading (or even trading while holding an item)
  • Evolving in / near an ingame area or object
  • Levelling up while having a specific move learned

      • How these evolution methods are done

      There are quite a few evolution methods as listed above. I will only be describing ones that are coloured green.

    • Leveling up - Exp. is obtained by defeating another pokemon in battle. The stronger the other pokemon is, the more experience obtained after the battle. After a set level, some pokemon will begin to evolve.
    • Using an item - Pokemon that evolve by using an item do not need to be at a certain level to evolve. Outside of battle, go into your bag and simply select the evolution item with a compatible pokemon to evolve it.
    • Trade - Trading is a core mechanic in all pokemon games that allows people to swap pokemon. If a pokemon can evolve via trading, it will begin its evolution while in the process of being traded.
