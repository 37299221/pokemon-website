Pokemon Items

Introduction

Items are a very important part of the pokemon games that are used throughout the whole series. Items can do a lot of things, such as evolving pokemon, healing pokemon, giving bonuses in battle, etc.

How to get items

Most items found in the games will be lying around in different places around each and every region. Items may also be bought from pokemarts, or in more recent games Pokecenters.

What items can do

  • Recovering HP or PP - Berries, potions, elixirs
  • Increasing damage in battle (directly or indirectly) - Choice band, black belt, razor fang
  • Removing status effects - Berries, healer items (burn heal, antidote, full heal, etc.)
  • Selling - Pearls, nuggets
  • Increasing friendship - Soothe bell
  • Learning moves - TMs, HMs, TRs
  • Game progression - Any key item (such as S.S. Anne ticket in Pokemon Red/Blue or bicycle for faster movement)
  • Gaining experience - Exp share, Exp candies
